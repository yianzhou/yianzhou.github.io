# App Analytics

[About app analytics](https://help.apple.com/app-store-connect/#/itc623752a8d)

Impressions（曝光）: The number of times your app was viewed in the Featured, Categories, Top Charts, and Search sections of the App Store. Includes Product Page Views.

Product Page Views: The number of times your app’s product page has been viewed on a device.

App Units（首次购买）: The number of first-time app downloads made on the App Store. App updates, downloads from the same Apple ID onto other devices, and redownloads to the same device are not counted.

❗️ Usage data includes **installations**, **sessions**, and **crashes**. App Analytics only shows data from users who have agreed to share their diagnostics and usage information with app developers. In the last 30 days, 23% of users that installed "GIF Maker – Video to..." agreed to share their data.

Installations（安装）: The total number of times your app has been installed on devices. Redownloads on the same device, downloads to multiple devices sharing the same Apple ID, and Family Sharing installations are included.

Sessions（活跃会话）: The number of times the app has been used for at least two seconds. If the app is in the background and is later used again, that counts as another session.

title:  "深入理解Swift协议"
categories: [Apple]
---

A protocol defines a blueprint of methods, properties, and other requirements that suit a particular task or piece of functionality. 协议是一个蓝图，定义了一些属性、方法去完成某种功能。

class、struct、enum可以实现某个协议，满足这些要求。协议的扩展可以为协议提供一些默认的实现。

A protocol can require any conforming type to provide an instance property or type property with a particular name and type. 协议可以要求遵循者提供某些属性。
